About RGS Interior Studio

RGS Interior Studio is a premium interior design firm based in Vadodara, Gujarat with over 35 years of experience transforming spaces into exceptional environments. We specialize in modular kitchens, custom furniture, and complete home interiors.

Our approach combines timeless design principles with contemporary aesthetics, delivering end-to-end solutions from concept to installation using only premium materials.

Design Excellence Contemporary designs with functional layouts
Premium Quality Only the finest materials and craftsmanship
End-to-End Execution Complete project management and installation
